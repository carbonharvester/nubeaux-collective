About

The talent is here. The representation isn't. We're changing that.

NUBEAUX Collective connects Africa's luxury hospitality properties with the continent's exceptional creators and creatives of colour.

The Opportunity

Heritage travel is one of the fastest-growing segments in luxury. Millions of people eager to explore their roots, reconnect with the continent, and experience Africa's finest properties. But when they see the marketing, they don't see themselves.

Most luxury properties still rely on marketing that lacks diversity — imagery, campaigns, and messaging that fails to resonate with this audience. It's not just a representation problem. It's a commercial blind spot.

NUBEAUX exists to close that gap. We uplift brands through elevated content, strategic creator campaigns, and digital transformation — connecting properties with the audiences they've been missing.

To transform African hospitality marketing by centering creative talent of colour — proving that authentic representation drives both cultural impact and commercial performance.
