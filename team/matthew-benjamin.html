Founder & Managing Director

Matthew Benjamin

Bridging luxury hospitality with untapped audiences.

Africa has always been home. After years working in luxury markets across Europe and the Middle East, Matthew returned with a clear mission: to connect the continent's finest properties with the creators and audiences they've been missing — and unlock the revenue they've been leaving on the table.

Email

The Problem Nobody Was Solving

After fifteen years building luxury brands across Europe and the Middle East — and earning an MBA in Luxury Management from the International University of Monaco — Matthew kept returning to the same question: why does Africa's hospitality marketing look nothing like Africa?

The pattern was impossible to ignore. World-class safari lodges, boutique hotels, and beach resorts across the continent — all marketing themselves with imagery that featured almost exclusively white guests. Meanwhile, the African diaspora — 200 million people with $4.2 trillion in spending power — were actively seeking luxury travel experiences on the continent. Heritage travel was booming. But the marketing wasn't speaking to them.

The disconnect wasn't malicious. It was institutional. Properties hired the same international agencies, worked with the same creator networks, and produced the same visual language that had defined African luxury tourism for decades. Nobody had built the infrastructure to connect them with creators of colour who could authentically reach diaspora audiences.

Matthew saw the gap — and the opportunity. Not just to address a representation problem, but to unlock genuine commercial value for properties willing to reach audiences their competitors couldn't see.

Building the Bridge

The Talent

Africa's Untapped Creators

Across the continent, extraordinary photographers, filmmakers, and content creators of colour were producing world-class work — but being overlooked by luxury hospitality brands defaulting to international agencies and familiar creator networks.

The Audience

The Diaspora Market

Heritage travel is the fastest-growing segment in African tourism. Diaspora travellers spend 40-60% more than traditional tourists. Yet almost no luxury properties were marketing directly to them — or in ways that resonated.

The Gap

Missing Infrastructure

Properties wanted to diversify their marketing but didn't know where to find vetted creators. Creators wanted luxury hospitality work but lacked access to decision-makers. Nobody had built the bridge between them.

The Solution

NUBEAUX Collective

A curated network of Africa's finest creators of colour, connected directly with luxury properties ready to reach new audiences. Not a marketplace — a collective built on relationships, quality, and commercial outcomes.

Philosophy

Representation isn't charity — it's commercial intelligence. The properties that authentically connect with diaspora audiences aren't just doing the right thing. They're accessing a market their competitors can't see. That's the opportunity we're building NUBEAUX to unlock.
