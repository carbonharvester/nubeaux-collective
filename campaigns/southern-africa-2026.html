Upcoming Campaign

Southern Africa

December 31, 2025 — January 18, 2026

An immersive 19-day exploration through five Southern African destinations, capturing the essence of luxury travel, cultural connection, and transformative experiences across South Africa, Namibia, Mozambique, and Botswana.

This campaign has been designed as a single, coherent narrative rather than a collection of isolated stays, with each destination building on the last. Participating properties are positioned within an integrated story that highlights atmosphere, service, design and sense of place. For partner hotels and lodges, this collaboration typically includes a dedicated in-feed Reel, story coverage throughout the stay, and thoughtfully crafted captions, alongside full usage rights in perpetuity for agreed assets.

Campaign Itinerary

Five destinations across Southern Africa, each building on the narrative of the last.

Destination 01

Cape Town

December 31 — January 5 · 5 nights

Cape Town opens the campaign with a strong sense of culture, heritage and coastal luxury. The focus in this chapter is on atmosphere, architecture, food and New Year energy.

Story themes

  • Heritage and design
  • New Year celebration moments
  • Wine and culinary culture
  • Coastline and city rhythm
Destination 02

Namibia

January 5 — January 8 · 3 nights

Namibia marks the shift into cinematic landscape storytelling. Here Nia is joined by filmmaker Klein Nettoh, whose expertise in wildlife and desert cinematography allows for premium, slow-paced visuals.

Story themes

  • Etosha National Park game drives
  • Onkolo Hide wildlife photography
  • Bush breakfast experiences
Destination 03

Johannesburg

January 8 — January 11 · 3 nights

Johannesburg introduces a contemporary African chapter focused on culture, art and modern city luxury. Klein Nettoh joins for this chapter, bringing cinematic production value to urban luxury storytelling.

Story themes

  • Art, design and modern culture
  • Urban luxury and local experiences
  • Contemporary food and lifestyle
Destination 04

Mozambique

January 11 — January 14 · 3 nights

Mozambique adds warmth, softness and ocean-led storytelling. This chapter includes coastal landscapes, island rhythm and sun-driven visuals.

Story themes

  • Ocean and island life
  • Slow pace and sunset storytelling
  • Marine experiences
Destination 05

Botswana

January 14 — January 18 · 4 nights

Botswana closes the campaign with a focus on wildlife and safari luxury. Klein Nettoh continues in this final chapter, offering elevated production value for properties seeking cinematic content.

Story themes

  • Wildlife and safari immersion
  • Delta and water-based storytelling
  • Conservation narratives
